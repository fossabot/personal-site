I recently went on a weekend kayaking fishing trip down at Rockport on the Texas coast with my dad. We chose [Goose Island State Park](http://tpwd.texas.gov/state-parks/goose-island), because we were familiar with the area and because you can stay right on the water.

> **Protip:** Not only does staying on the water allow you to fish from your campsite, the constant breeze can also help keep the mosquitoes away.

It didn't take long to realize our planned expeditions were a bit on the ambitious side in terms of distance covered in the accounted timespan. The main thing we didn't plan for at the time were the wind conditions, both direction and speed, which have a large effect on the distance you can cover and drift directions.
